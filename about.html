Our Story

Local Expertise. National-Scale Results.

Graebeck Construction Ltd. was founded at the turn of the millennium in North Gower, Ontario, a small farming community just south of Ottawa with a straightforward work ethic and no appetite for shortcuts. That ethos has never changed, even as the projects have grown dramatically in scale and complexity.

Over two decades, we have assembled a team of senior project managers, experienced site superintendents, and trade specialists who have delivered some of the most demanding construction projects in federal Canada. From Parliament Hill's heritage masonry envelope to critical health-care infrastructure at the Civic Hospital campus | Graebeck's name has come to represent precision, reliability, and trust.

We are deliberately focused on the National Capital Region. Deep local knowledge of Ottawa's soil conditions, heritage overlay designations, federal procurement frameworks, and municipal permit processes gives our clients a tangible advantage on every project. We know this market, and our track record proves it.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Safety Without Compromise

Every worker goes home the same way they arrived. Our COR-certified safety program, daily site inspections, and zero-tolerance culture have maintained an exemplary safety record across all active sites.

Transparent Communication

No surprises. Our clients receive consistent, honest reporting on schedule, cost, and risk. Good news or complicated news, clients always hear it straight. Trust is earned through candour, not spin.

Environmental Stewardship

We build for future generations. Sustainable practices, waste diversion, responsible sourcing, and LEED certification support are embedded in our standard site operations. They are not optional extras.

Local Expertise

Two decades of project delivery in the National Capital Region means deep familiarity with Ottawa's regulatory landscape, sub-trade community, heritage designations, and federal procurement processes.

People-First Culture

Long-tenured site superintendents, loyal sub-trade relationships, and a low turnover team mean continuity and craft that clients feel on every project. We invest in our people as much as our projects.

Accountability & Delivery

Schedule is a commitment, not a suggestion. Our project management discipline, built on detailed look-ahead schedules, rigorous sub-trade coordination, and proactive risk identification, keeps projects on track.

Community Roots

Proudly Based in North Gower

Our company address at 6361 Fourth Line Rd, North Gower, is not just a mailing address. It's where we were founded, where many of our team members live, and where our values were formed. The rural Ottawa Valley work ethic: show up, do it right, stand behind your work. It's as much a part of Graebeck's DNA as any certification or award.

We are active supporters of local trade apprenticeship programs and community organizations across Rideau-Carleton. Strengthening the community that built us is a responsibility we take seriously.
