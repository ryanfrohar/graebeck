About Graebeck

We are Graebeck Construction Ltd., one of Ottawa's most trusted construction firms.

Founded in 2000, Graebeck Construction Ltd. has grown to become one of Ottawa's largest construction firms. Our projects range from major multi-million dollar structural upgrades on Parliament Hill to smaller-scale renovations and everything in between.

Our company philosophy is simple: we focus on providing superior service and workmanship. This commitment has earned us a reputation as the "go-to" experts for near-impossible construction tasks. We achieve this by utilizing the latest project management technology, leveraging strong relationships with experienced local tradesmen, and maintaining an unwavering commitment to employee safety.

We are also deeply committed to our industry and are proud members of numerous local construction associations.
