Our Story

Ottawa Roots.
National-Scale Results.

Graebeck Construction is known for delivering complex projects with control, accountability, and consistent results.

Since 2000, we have built a reputation for executing complex work across Ottawa and the surrounding region, from major structural upgrades on Parliament Hill to specialized industrial, institutional, and restoration projects.

We approach every project with a disciplined focus on coordination, risk management, and execution. By combining experienced leadership, trusted trade partnerships, and proven project management systems, we maintain control over schedule, cost, and quality from start to finish. This consistent, accountable approach has earned us a reputation for delivering complex projects reliably, with a strong emphasis on safety and well-managed job sites.

We stay actively engaged in the construction industry through ongoing involvement with local associations and industry partners.

Graebeck is trusted to deliver when it matters most.
