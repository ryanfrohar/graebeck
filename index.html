Our Story

North Gower Roots.
National-Scale Results.

Founded in 2000 and headquartered in North Gower, Ontario, Graebeck Construction Ltd. has grown from a trusted local contractor into one of the National Capital Region's most respected general contractors. Our portfolio spans federal landmark restorations, critical health-care infrastructure, and landmark commercial developments.

What sets us apart is not just the scale of our work — it's the discipline we bring to every project. A commitment to transparent communication, rigorous safety protocols, and sustainable building practices is baked into every estimate, every schedule, and every final walkthrough.
