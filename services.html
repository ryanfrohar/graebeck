Design Build

From Concept to Completion

Graebeck's design-build service delivers a fully integrated solution from project inception through to occupancy. A single point of accountability spans site selection, outline specifications, design development, construction, fit-out, and commissioning | streamlining the process and reducing risk for the owner.

By combining design and construction responsibility under one contract, design-build shortens project timelines, enables early cost certainty, and fosters collaboration between the design team and trades from day one.

  • Site Selection & Outline Specifications
  • Integrated Design Development
  • Single Point of Accountability
  • Accelerated Project Delivery
  • Fit-Out & Commissioning
